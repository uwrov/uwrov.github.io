Welcome to the UWROV Club

About Us

Underwater Remotely Operated Vehicle (UWROV) is an undergraduate University of Washington club based in Seattle. We design, build, and use ROVs for research, as well as to compete in the Marine Advanced Technology and Education (MATE) competition. We were founded in 2011, and in the 2014 MATE International competition, we placed 12th. In addition, we also participate in research endeavors such as assisting the Exploration and Remote Instrumentation by Students (ERIS) class deploy sensors for the freshwater underwater observatory.
