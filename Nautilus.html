The Nautilus and US

In 2018-19 UWROV has a team of thirteen undergraduate students. We spend hours together in the room adorned with aluminum and silicon parts, aiming to bring Nautilus to life. We have members of the computer science major focusing on coding the Arduino and Raspberry Pi, the central computers of Nautilus. We have members talented in electrical and mechanical engineering, wiring and planning the frame of our ROV, and adjusting the motors to ensure precision. We are proud to present you the snapshots of our advancements.
