Regional Competition

The Marine Advanced Technology and Education (MATE) Center supports their regional Pacific Northwest held in Washington. The missions change annually and reflect current events that occur in the area hosting the international competition. The regional acts as a qualifier for the international competition.

2014 Competition

  • Vehicle: Orcus
  • Location: Federal Way, Washington
  • Missions based on shipwrecks
  • Qualified for the 2014 International MATE Competition

2015 Competition

  • Vehicle: Peter Pan
  • Location: Federal Way, Washington
  • Missions based on science and industry
  • Dates: May 9, 2015
